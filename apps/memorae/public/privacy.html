Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

1. Introduction

Jarvis AI Assistant ("Jarvis", "we", "us") is an AI-powered assistant service operated by WiPay Caribbean. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with Jarvis via WhatsApp or other messaging platforms.

2. Information We Collect

When you use Jarvis, we may collect:

  • Phone number — your WhatsApp phone number, used to identify your account
  • Messages — text, voice notes, images, and documents you send to Jarvis
  • Usage data — timestamps, message counts, and feature usage for service improvement

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the Jarvis AI assistant service
  • To maintain conversation context and memory for personalized responses
  • To process reminders, briefings, and scheduled tasks you create
  • To process payments through WiPay for premium features

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on encrypted servers. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information. Conversation data is stored locally and is not shared with third parties except as necessary to provide the service (e.g., AI processing via Anthropic, speech-to-text via OpenAI Whisper).

5. Third-Party Services

Jarvis uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Meta/WhatsApp Business API — messaging platform
  • Anthropic (Claude) — AI language processing
  • OpenAI (Whisper) — voice note transcription
  • ElevenLabs — text-to-speech
  • WiPay — payment processing

6. Data Retention

We retain your conversation data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by messaging Jarvis or contacting us directly.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your stored data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of the service at any time

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at awayne@jarvisproject.ai.

9. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of Jarvis after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
