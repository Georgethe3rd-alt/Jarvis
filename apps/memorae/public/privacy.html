Privacy Policy

// Data Protection Protocol
Last updated: March 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how J.A.R.V.I.S. ("we", "the Service") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal data. We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with applicable data protection laws including GDPR.

1. Data We Collect

Data TypeWhatWhy
Registration DataName, email, WhatsApp phone numberAccount creation and communication
WhatsApp MessagesText messages, voice notes, images, documents you send to JarvisTo provide AI assistant functionality
Conversation HistoryYour messages and AI responsesContext for personalized assistance

2. How We Use Your Data

  • AI Processing: Your messages are sent to AI language models (currently Anthropic Claude) to generate responses. Messages are processed in real-time and are not used to train AI models.
  • Memory & Personalization: Information you share is stored in your isolated workspace to provide personalized assistance over time.
  • Reminders & Briefings: Your scheduled items are processed to deliver timely notifications via WhatsApp.
  • Service Improvement: Aggregated, anonymized usage statistics help us improve the Service.
  • Communication: Your email may be used for account-related notifications (activation codes, plan changes, important service updates).

3. Data Storage & Security

  • Your data is stored on secured servers with encrypted connections.
  • Each user has an isolated workspace — your data is completely separated from other users.
  • Database backups are created regularly and encrypted at rest.
  • Access to user data is restricted to authorized administrators only.
  • We use industry-standard security measures including HTTPS, password hashing, JWT authentication, and rate limiting.

4. Third-Party Data Sharing

We share data only with the following third parties, strictly as needed to provide the Service:

  • Meta (WhatsApp Business API): Your messages pass through WhatsApp's infrastructure as part of normal messaging.
  • Anthropic: Your messages are sent to Anthropic's AI API for processing. Anthropic's data policies apply to API usage.
  • ElevenLabs (optional): If voice features are used, text is sent for speech synthesis.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers or data brokers.

5. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data is retained as long as your account is active.
  • Inactive accounts: Accounts inactive for 12 months may be flagged for deletion after notification.
  • Deleted accounts: Upon account deletion, all personal data is permanently removed within 30 days.
  • Backups: Backup copies are automatically purged after 7 days.

6. Your Rights

Under GDPR and applicable data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Restriction: Request limitation of processing in certain circumstances.
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data.
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time by deactivating your account.

To exercise any of these rights, contact the administrator or send "delete my data" to Jarvis via WhatsApp.

7. Data Deletion

You can request complete data deletion at any time. This includes:

  • All conversation history
  • All stored memories and notes
  • All reminders and briefing settings
  • Your workspace files (SOUL.md, MEMORY.md, daily notes)
  • Your account and registration data

Deletion is permanent and irreversible. Processing takes up to 30 days to ensure removal from all backups.

8. Cookies & Tracking

The web interface uses only essential technical storage (JWT tokens for authentication). We do not use tracking cookies, analytics scripts, or advertising pixels.

9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for users under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

11. Contact

For privacy inquiries or data requests, contact the administrator through the Service or via the registration email on file.
